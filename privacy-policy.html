Privacy Policy

Screen Goated Toolbox — mobile application
Last updated: 31 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Screen Goated Toolbox mobile application (“the App”) handles information. The App is a personal-productivity toolkit that provides features such as speech recognition, translation, text-to-speech, live translation, content generation, and media downloading. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is used.

1. We do not operate a data-collection server

The developer of the App does not run any server that collects, stores, or sells your personal information. The App has no advertising and no analytics SDKs that profile you. Your content is not sent to the developer.

2. Data you provide is processed by third-party AI services you choose

Many features work by sending the input you provide to third-party artificial-intelligence and cloud services that you configure using your own accounts and API keys. Depending on which features you use, the following content may be transmitted to those providers in order to fulfil your request:

  • Audio you record (for speech recognition, translation, or text-to-speech)
  • Audio and/or screen content captured while you actively run a Live Translate session
  • Text, prompts, and files that you choose to process
  • Media URLs you enter for downloading

Examples of providers the App can connect to include Google Gemini, Groq, Cerebras, OpenRouter, and self-hosted endpoints such as Ollama. Each provider processes your data under its own privacy policy and terms. You decide which providers to enable, and no data is sent to a provider unless you have configured it and triggered the feature.

3. API keys and settings

Any API keys, endpoints, and preferences you enter are stored locally on your device using the operating system’s secure storage. They are used solely to authenticate your requests to the providers you have chosen. They are never transmitted to the developer and are removed when you delete them or uninstall the App.

4. Device permissions

  • Microphone — to capture speech for recognition, translation, and text-to-speech features that you start.
  • Screen capture / media projection — used only while you actively start a Live Translate session, to translate on-screen content or device audio. Capture stops when you end the session.
  • Notifications — to display the status of an ongoing foreground session.
  • Display over other apps — to show the optional floating translation overlay.
  • Internet access — to communicate with the third-party services you configure.

5. Data sharing

Your content is shared only with the third-party providers you have explicitly configured, and only to carry out the action you requested. We do not sell your data and do not share it for advertising. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

6. Data retention

The App does not retain your content on any developer-controlled server. Content you send to third-party providers is retained and deleted according to those providers’ own policies. Locally stored settings remain on your device until you remove them or uninstall the App.

7. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We rely on your device’s secure storage to protect locally stored keys and settings, and on encrypted (HTTPS) connections for communication with third-party providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your choices

You control which providers and keys the App uses. You can edit or delete your keys and settings at any time within the App, and uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
