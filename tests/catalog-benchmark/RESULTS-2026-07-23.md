## Catalog Decisions

- Remove `scout` and `text_qwen3_32b`.
- Do not place Groq Llama 3.3 in a default chain because its provider shutdown
  is scheduled for 2026-08-16.
- Image-to-text order: Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite, Google Gemma 4 31B, Gemini 3.1
  Flash-Lite, Cerebras Gemma 4 31B, Google Gemma 4 26B, Qwen 3.6.
- Text-to-text order: Google Gemma 4 31B, Groq GPT-OSS 120B, Gemini 3.5
  Flash-Lite, Cerebras GLM 4.7, Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite, Cerebras GPT-OSS 120B,
  Gemini Live 3.1.
- Brain tiers express successful-output capability. Availability, latency
  variance, quota exhaustion, and endpoint lifecycle separately determine
  names and retry order.
